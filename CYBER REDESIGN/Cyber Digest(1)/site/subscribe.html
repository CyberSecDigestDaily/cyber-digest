Frequently asked.

Why is the Free tier so generous?

Because the daily wrap is the product, and an undefended internet is bad for our paying customers too. We monetise the operational layer — alerts, feeds, briefings — rather than the news itself.

Do you accept advertising or sponsored placement?

No. Our editorial policy at about.html#standards documents the firewall. We disclose every enterprise contract above £25,000/yr at legal.html#disclosures.

How is the IOC feed delivered?

STIX 2.1 over TAXII 2.1, with JSON and CSV mirrors for teams that prefer them. Authentication via API key on Practitioner, mTLS on Enterprise. Documented at docs.cyberdigest.io.

Can I expense this?

Yes. Invoices are emailed on day-of-charge with VAT-registered (number published at launch). PO billing and net-30 terms are available on the Enterprise plan.

What's your data-retention & privacy posture?

We store the minimum required to deliver the product. Cookies are functional only — no third-party analytics ad-tech, no behavioural retargeting. Detail at legal.html#privacy.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Self-serve in the billing portal. Annual subscribers receive a pro-rata refund within 30 days of renewal.

Do you offer student / non-profit pricing?

Yes — free Practitioner access for accredited students and 70% off for registered non-profits. Mail billing@cyberdigest.io with a letterheaded request.
