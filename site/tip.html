Before you send anything

  • Use a personal device, not a work one. Corporate endpoints are routinely instrumented.
  • Use a network you don't own — café Wi-Fi, mobile hotspot — and consider a VPN.
  • Strip metadata from documents and images. exiftool -all= on a copy, never the original.
  • If your role limits how directly you can talk, send a one-line introduction and a callback method that works for you.
  • If the story involves your employer, please consult your jurisdiction's whistleblower-protection guidance first.
